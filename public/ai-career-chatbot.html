AI Career Chatbot

If you've checked out my homepage, you've seen my chatbot. It's an AI assistant that answers questions about my background using my LinkedIn profile (parsed from a PDF) and a summary. It uses OpenAI's function calling with two tools: one captures leads when someone shares their email, and another logs questions it can't answer. Both send push notifications to my phone in real time, so I know when someone's interested or when there's a gap in my knowledge base. Built with OpenAI's API, Gradio, and Pushover. It's essentially an automated resume presenter and lead capture system in one :)
