My fave movies

This is a list of my top 3 movies

Spirited Away

There is something very liberating about this movie.
The fantasy creatures and scenery and new way of life allow
you to really disconnect and enjoy the adventure.

Romulus

I'm a complete sci-fi nerd and it does not really get
better than this. There is new tech and dystopia galor,
great way for you to fantasize about the future and potential
of humanity.

Catch me if you can

A feel good movie about a criminal turned nice guy. Leonardo
raises himself out of poverty by way of forgery and is caught
by a relentless cop. Friendship ensues and leaves you feeling
like behind every bad turn is an opportunity for redemption.
