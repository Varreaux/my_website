Perceptron and Adaline Classifiers

From‑scratch implementations of Perceptron and Adaline binary classifiers trained on the Iris dataset. Explores both linearly separable (Iris-setosa vs. others) and non‑linearly separable (versicolor vs. virginica) classification tasks across different feature combinations. Achieved 92–100% test accuracy depending on data separability, with Adaline demonstrating superior convergence through gradient descent optimization. Includes visualizations of training progress, decision boundaries, and comparative analysis of learning rates, feature importance, and model convergence behavior.
