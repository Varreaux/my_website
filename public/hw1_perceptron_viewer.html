Perceptron and Adaline

I built Perceptron and Adaline from scratch and trained both on the Iris dataset across linearly separable and non-separable feature combinations. My full code is in the notebook further down the page. Both models learn a linear decision boundary, but their weight updates come from very different signals and the contrast is much easier to see than to describe. So I put together the playground below to train both on the same points at once and watch the two boundaries diverge.
